Furniture Assembly FAQ

How long does furniture assembly take?

Simple items like a bookshelf take 30-45 minutes. A full bedroom set (bed, dresser, nightstands) typically takes 2-3 hours. We will give you a time estimate when you book.

Do I need to unbox the furniture first?

No. We handle everything from unboxing to assembly to cleanup. Just show us where you want it placed.

Can you assemble furniture I bought from any store?

Yes. We assemble furniture from any store or brand. If it comes in a box with instructions, we can build it.

What if parts are missing?

We will identify the missing parts and help you contact the manufacturer for replacements. If it is a common hardware piece, we may have a spare in our truck.
