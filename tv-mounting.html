TV Mounting FAQ

How long does TV mounting take?

Most TV mounting jobs take 45 to 90 minutes, depending on wall type and cable concealment requirements. Concrete and brick walls take a bit longer due to drilling.

Do I need to provide the mount bracket?

You can provide your own bracket or we can bring one. We will recommend the right type (fixed, tilting, or full-motion) based on your TV size and viewing preferences.

Can you mount a TV on a concrete wall?

Yes. Many Miami condos and apartments have concrete walls. We bring concrete-rated anchors and masonry drill bits specifically for this. No problem at all.

What about cable management?

We always include basic external cable management. For a cleaner look, we offer in-wall cable concealment starting at $75. This hides all cables inside the wall for a seamless, showroom appearance.

Do you mount TVs above fireplaces?

Yes, we do. We will also advise on heat considerations and the best viewing angle for above-fireplace installations.
