General Handyman FAQ

Can I give you a list of multiple tasks?

Absolutely. Many customers give us a list of 5-10 small tasks and we knock them all out in one visit. This is actually the most cost-effective way to use our service — you get more value from the minimum and save on separate trip charges.

What is the minimum charge?

Our minimum is a 2-hour booking at $80/hour ($160 total). For single small tasks, we have a flat-rate minimum of $79. Either way, we will tell you the cost upfront before we start.

Do you work in condos and apartments?

Yes. We work in single-family homes, condos, townhomes, and apartments throughout Miami-Dade County. We are familiar with building access procedures and HOA requirements common in Miami.

Do you offer recurring maintenance?

Yes. Many property managers and homeowners book us for monthly or quarterly maintenance visits. Contact us for a custom maintenance plan and discounted rates.
