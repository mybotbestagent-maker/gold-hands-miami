Electrical FAQ

Do you handle major electrical work?

We focus on minor electrical tasks. For panel upgrades, new circuit installation, or whole-house rewiring, we recommend a licensed electrician and can refer you to one.

What is a GFCI outlet and do I need one?

A GFCI (Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter) outlet protects against electrical shock near water. Florida code requires them in kitchens, bathrooms, garages, outdoor areas, and laundry rooms. If your home has old outlets in these areas, upgrading is both a safety and code requirement.

Can you add USB outlets?

Yes. We replace standard outlets with USB-integrated ones so you can charge phones and tablets directly from the wall. Very popular upgrade — takes about 15 minutes per outlet.
