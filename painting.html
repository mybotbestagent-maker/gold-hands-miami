Painting FAQ

How long does it take to paint a room?

A standard room typically takes one day including prep, two coats, and cleanup. Larger rooms or complex jobs may take 1.5-2 days. We will give you an exact timeline with your quote.

Do I need to provide the paint?

You can provide your own paint, or we can purchase it for you at our contractor discount and pass the savings along. No markup on materials.

How do I choose the right color?

We can help. We will bring sample cards or paint swatches. Pro tip: test a small section first and look at it in both daylight and evening light before committing.

Do you paint ceilings?

Yes, ceiling painting is available as an add-on. We recommend a flat/matte finish for ceilings to hide imperfections.
