Drywall Repair FAQ

How long does drywall repair take?

Small patches can be done in 1-2 hours. Larger repairs or multiple holes may take half a day. Note that joint compound needs drying time — we may need a second visit for sanding and primer on larger jobs.

Do you paint the repaired area?

We include primer on all repairs. Full painting is available as an add-on service. We can match your existing wall color or apply your chosen color.

Can you fix water-damaged drywall?

Yes. We cut out the damaged section, install new drywall, tape, mud, and finish. However, make sure the water source is fixed first — we do not want to repair the same area twice.

Do you handle ceiling repairs?

Absolutely. We repair ceiling drywall, including sagging areas, water stains, and holes from old fixtures.
