FAQ

Can you install a ceiling fan where there is currently a light fixture?

In most cases, yes. We will verify the electrical box can support the fan weight and upgrade it if needed. This may add a small cost but ensures safe operation.

Do you install recessed lighting?

Yes. We install recessed (can) lights in existing ceilings. Pricing depends on the number of lights and whether new wiring is needed.

How long does installation take?

A simple light swap takes about 30 minutes. Ceiling fan installation typically takes 1-2 hours. Chandeliers can take up to 2 hours depending on size and weight.
