Smart Home FAQ

Do I need to buy the smart devices myself?

You can provide your own devices, or we can pick them up for you at retail price with no markup. We will recommend the best options for your needs and budget.

Will it work with my existing WiFi?

Most smart devices work with standard home WiFi. We will test signal strength at the installation location and advise if you need a WiFi extender.

Can you install outdoor cameras?

Yes. We install outdoor security cameras with proper weatherproofing and cable routing. We can also set up motion zones and notification alerts.

Do you set up voice control?

Yes. We connect your devices to Alexa, Google Home, or Siri and test voice commands before we leave.
